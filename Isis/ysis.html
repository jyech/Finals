He Who is Ysis

Ysis De Elmir

He is characterized as possessing striking handsomeness and serves as a beacon of hope within his realm. With a resplendent mane of golden locks and eyes resembling the brilliance of emeralds, he consistently dons regal garments befitting his role as the nation's prince. His demeanor exudes warmth and affability, and he displays a notable inclination toward safeguarding his sister, Aisha.

His Mother

Iris De Elmis

Iris De Elmir is Ysis's stepmother, and the previous queen passed away due to illness. It's important to mention that Ysis loves his stepmom just as much as his biological mom. Iris genuinely takes care of Ysis as if he were her own child.

His Sister

Aisha De Elmir

Ysis has held a deep and unwavering affection for his sister ever since the day she was born. From the very moment he first laid eyes on her, a profound sense of protectiveness and love enveloped his heart. He eagerly embraced the role of a caring and doting sibling, always eager to lend a hand and offer support. Their bond is a testament to the enduring connection between siblings, one that has only grown stronger with the passage of time

His Father

Tyreon De Elmir

Ysis's father, Tyreon, may appear to be a stern and imposing figure when seen from the outside. However, within the confines of their family circle, he reveals a heart of gold. Despite his formidable exterior, Tyreon is exceedingly sweet and affectionate towards his loved ones. He prioritizes their well-being and happiness, often going to great lengths to ensure their comfort and security. This stark contrast between his public and private personas showcases the depth of his love and commitment to his family.
